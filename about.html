Our Story

LOTSTE was founded in 2023 on the belief that the biggest hurdles in aerospace and robotics are opportunities for groundbreaking innovation. Our journey began in a small lab with a shared mission: to build smarter, more efficient, and more reliable systems. Today, we've grown into a dynamic team that continues to push the boundaries of what's possible, turning ambitious ideas into tangible realities.
