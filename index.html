Featured Projects

A snapshot of platforms and technologies we are advancing.

Model-Based UAV System with an authoritative digital surrogate

The traditional lifecycle of unmanned systems is being fundamentally redefined. Through the application of Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE), our platforms are virtually perfected prior to manufacturing, eliminating design risks. Once deployed, each asset operates in tandem with an authoritative digital surrogate, creating a closed-loop system that enables predictive maintenance, continuous learning, and a new standard of operational intelligence.

Airborne Communications Relay

Our Airborne Communications Relay is engineered to provide temporary infrastructure by packaging a complete network node into a rapidly deployable UAV. This system is designed to provide on-demand 4G/5G and Wi-Fi coverage, establishing a high-bandwidth 'network in the sky' in under 30 minutes. It serves as an agile solution for everything from public event overload to critical operations in infrastructure-denied zones.

GNSS/INS-denied rocket navigation

Project Ghost addresses the most catastrophic guidance failure scenario: the simultaneous loss of both GNSS and INS data. In this event, our system is engineered to resurrect its sense of direction by reconstructing a missile's complete state vector using only non-inertial, environmental-sensing techniques. It achieves this by treating the surrounding environment as a complex map of visual, magnetic, and radio landmarks, allowing it to re-establish its position and heading from absolute scratch. This fundamental shift from internal dead-reckoning to external environmental awareness creates a profoundly resilient system capable of surviving and completing its mission even after a total loss of its primary senses.
